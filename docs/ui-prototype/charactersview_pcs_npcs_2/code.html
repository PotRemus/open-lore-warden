Chronicles & Background

"The light is not a shield, it is a burden that must be carried until the dark is no more."

Born in the Silvermarches, Thalric was once a rising star in the Order of the Radiant Sun. However, during the Siege of Mournhaven, he witnessed the order's leadership choose military gain over the lives of civilians. Breaking his oath, he slew his commander and led the refugees to safety.

Now a wanderer, he carries his cracked sun-shield as a reminder of the day he became an oathbreaker in the eyes of the law, but a hero in the eyes of the forgotten.
